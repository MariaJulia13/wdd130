Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share information, share the experiences of the most experienced adventurers with those who are discovering the interest in the sport, and help assemble teams to practice it.

Audience

The audience of Atlantis will be adventurers of more than 18 years who want to escape a little from the reality of life, or simply laugh and know different points of view of life. They will be able to have this contact with other adventurers both on the laptop and on the mobile.
